    Design is my passion! From the early years of highschool I have liked everything about Design. One of the first classes I took that got me on the path I am today was titled Graphic Arts. It was an introductory class that taught us how to use photoshop and while it was very challenging I was determined to master the craft. I ended up taking that class again so that I could refine my skills only to learn that I was still very much scrathing the surface at the world of Visual Communication Design. I have come a long way from there taking many classes ranging from Motion Design to Web Design.
