    • About Me

    For my whole life I have lived in Washingtong State. Up until the age of 18 I lived and went to school in Edmonds, Washington. When the fall of '16 came, I moved to Cheney, WA where I currently study at Eastern Washington University.

    While in college I have taken many classes in the Visual Communication field. I have become proficient in many programs such as Illustrater, Photoshop, InDesign, After Effects, Studio One and more. These all serve as tools for myself as an always evolving designer who wants to connect the world through design.
